Some Common Problems One Might Encounter Using Smile Network

How do I recharge roaming data?

The following steps can help you recharge your Smile roaming data:

  • Make sure you have the SmileKonnect App installed (available from the App or Google Play Store)
  • To purchase a package, go to the menu button and pick “Buy Package.”
  • Enter the serial number of the device (this can be found at the back of the device)
  • Review and accept the terms and conditions, then click pay to complete your transaction
  • Verify your PayPal payment

I don’t know how to use the Smile Konnect device

  • Press the power button to turn on the SmileKonnect device
  • Wait for the Internet Connection/4G LTE LED lights to turn green before proceeding. This shows that the item is ready to use and is linked to a local network
  • With your device, look for the SmileKonnect WiFi connection settings (SSID on the back of the device) (phone, tablet, laptop, etc.)
  • When prompted, choose the connection and enter the password (found on the back of the device)
  • The SmileKonnect gadget generates a simple-to-use wifi hotspot that can receive 4G LTE signals

How can I activate SmileVoice to make voice calls?

Smile clients may download the FREE SmileVoice App for Android and iOS devices from the Google Play or App Store and use it to make limitless FREE on-net (Smile-to-Smile) calls. Customers may now use a Smile SIM card to make calls on their compatible VoLTE feature/smartphone.

Why are all of my Smile devices linked to the Smile 4G LTE network ringing when I receive a call?

Yes, when you get a call, it will ring on all of your SmileVoice-enabled devices that are connected to a data network at the moment. Both devices will ring if you have activated SmileVoice on your Android phone (by installing the app) and your VoLTE phone and both are connected to Smile’s 4G LTE network or another data provider. Disconnect one to prevent such problems from occurring and to secure your network conversation.

As a new user, I find it difficult to sign into my Smile network App

Please follow the procedures below if you are a first-time user who has not yet logged into the MySmile Portal:

  • On your mobile device, download and install the MySmile App from the appropriate device store
  • Open the app
  • Tap ‘Register’
  • Type in your SmileNumber or username, which was emailed to you when you signed up for Smile. Please call Smile Customer Care at 0702 444 4444 if you are unsure about your SmileNumber or Username
  • After you’ve input your SmileNumber or username, choose a memorable password and enter it
  • Give your consent to the terms of service and Privacy Policy
  • To proceed, click SUBMIT
  • To get a 6-digit OTP, enter your registered mobile number (GSM number)
  • To verify, enter the OTP received on your registered mobile number and click VERIFY
  • You’ll be sent to the MySmile App’s home screen after completing the registration process

How do I share airtime with Smile numbers linked to my account?

Log into the MySmile App or MySmile Portal with your SmileNumber/Username and Password to share airtime amongst SmileNumbers tied to your account. Then, on the share icon, pick Airtime as well as your profile’s connected SmileNumber. You’ll get a notice confirming that airtime was successfully transferred. You may also examine the airtime balance in the SmileNumber you shared by going to Balances after sharing.

How do I restrict access to my Smile network router?

The router may be set up to block access based on IP, MAC address, and URL. A laptop/desktop can also be denied a Wi-Fi connection to the router. Please contact our Customer Care Center at (toll-free) 0702 044 4444 or send an email to [email protected] for more information on how to put this up.

I find it difficult to know when my router is connected to the PC

When the LED light on the router’s ‘Mode’ symbol blinks blue and your computer’s ‘LAN’ icon or wireless SSID highlights the ‘connected’ state, your computer is connected to the router. When the router’s ‘Mode’ symbol is flashing red, it means there’s no connectivity, and when it’s blinking white, it means the router is trying to connect to the wireless network.

How Can I Access My Balances On The Smile Network

Any of the following methods can be used to check the balance of your airtime, data, and voice services:

  • The MySmile App (download it on your phone and sign in with your SmileNumber/Username and password to check your balance)
  • The MySmile Portal (go to smile.com.ng and click on LOGIN, then enter your SmileNumber/Username and Password to check your balance)
  • Send “Y” to 343 from SmileVoice or your registered mobile number to check your voice and data balance

I experience constant disconnection and low network

Check for any items in the area that have magnetic fields, such as microwaves, ovens, and disc satellite antennae, and move the router away from them. While checking the signal indication bar on your router, try moving it to a different spot. To have a pleasant browsing experience, you’ll need at least two signal bars. Please contact Smile Customer Care at 0702 044 4444 (toll-free) or 0704 645 0000 for additional assistance and troubleshooting guidance, or send an email to [email protected] and we’ll call you back.

How do I reset my Smile 4G LTE password?

To switch from the default password to one you like, follow these steps:

  • Connect to the SMILE Wi-Fi using the default password and the modem’s Wi-Fi Key (WPA/WPA2)
  • Open your web browser
  • Go to http://homerouter.cpe/ or 20192.168.8.1 or 192.168.1.1 to get started
  • Use your username and password to log in. You can put anything as a username and password if you haven’t updated them before. (NOTE that you may never be able to change the username in the future, so type carefully)
  • On certain models, the default username and password are user and admin, respectively
  • Go to Settings > Wi-Fi > Wi-Fi Basic Settings after logging in
  • You may alter your SSID, Hide SSID broadcast, Wi-Fi password, and other options under Wi-Fi Basic Settings
  • When you’re finished, click Apply
