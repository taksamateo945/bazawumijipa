Why Did Adrienne Pedersen Leave The Network? Adrienne Pederson, a morning news co-anchor at WISN-TV since 2018, has announced her departure from the ABC network and television news.

Adrienne is the host of “UPFRONT,” a local news show that airs in Milwaukee and Wisconsin. She also contributes to WISN 12 News This Morning as a co-anchor.

Before moving to Wisconsin, Pederson worked as a reporter for WFLA-TV in Tampa, Florida. Adrienne covered major events such as the Republican National Convention in 2012.

Adrienne graduated from the University of Missouri’s Summa Cum Laude program with a bachelor’s degree in journalism. She was born and raised in Naperville, Illinois, and her parents live in Wisconsin.

Why Did Adrienne Pedersen Leave The Network?

As per jsonline, Adrienne Pedersen left WISN 12 network to seek her career outside the television.

However, she hasn’t figured out her next working destination. She stated that she wanted a change for a long time.

She is currently enjoying her “Me Time” after working with the same schedule and routines for more than five years.

Adrienne shared pictures of her adorable son Harrison Pedersen on her official Instagram account.

Where Is Adrienne Pedersen Going From WISN 12?

Adrienne Pedersen hasn’t said where she’ll go now that she’s left the WISN 12 network.

Pedersen will be replaced as co-anchor of This Morning by Gerron Jordan, who joined Channel 12’s morning news crew as a live desk anchor in November.

Pedersen joined Channel 12 in 2016 as a weekday reporter and weekend evening co-anchor. She was promoted to co-anchor of the weekday morning news in 2018.

She was upgraded to host of “UPFRONT” the station’s Sunday morning public affairs program airing in Milwaukee and Wisconsin, which was previously hosted by Mike Gousha.

Adrienne Pedersen’s Husband Revealed

Adrienne Pedersen’s Twitter posts indicate that she is married. The pair tied their knot in January 2007. 

However, she hasn’t shared her husband’s name in the media portals. Adrienne regularly uploads pictures with her husband.

The couple is blessed with a baby boy in March 2021 named Harrison Pedersen. Her guest host Math Smith broke the good news to the public.
