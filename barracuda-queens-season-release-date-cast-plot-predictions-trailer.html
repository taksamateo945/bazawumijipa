Barracuda Queens Season 1 Plot: What Can it be About?

Barracuda Queens loosely draws from true events, following a group of young women in the 1990s who rebelliously commit thefts seeking thrills and vengeance against men who wronged them. Set in 1995 during the empowering “girl power” era, Lollo, Klara, Frida, and Mia, privileged Djursholm girls, embark on a risky plan to rob their neighbor Amina after a hefty party bill.

As their burglaries escalate, they juggle their daytime roles as accomplished students and daughters while becoming ruthless thieves at night. Their daring exploits unfold with secrets hidden, leaving viewers to wonder if they’ll ever be suspected.
