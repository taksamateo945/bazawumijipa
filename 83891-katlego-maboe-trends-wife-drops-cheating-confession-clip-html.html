Katlego Maboe has been trending on social media after his wife Monique Muller shared a video exposing that he had cheated on her and given her a STD (sexually transmitted disease).

Monique found out that Katlego was cheating when she was diagnosed with a STD that is degrading the tissue of her womb. Knowing that she had not been with anyone but Katlego, Monique knew her husband had been unfaithful.

Confronting him with the news, Monique filmed the whole conversation and ousted Katlego on social media. Monique made Katlego say the woman’s name, Nikita Murray, in the video and it has since been revealed that the woman he cheated on Monique with is also married.

The clip is extremely personal and emotional and some feel Monique was out of line posting it on social media; however, others feel she did the right thing.

Katlego has since come forward to admit guilt and tell his side of the story. Seeing Katlego’s confession has made a lot of people angry as they feel he is now playing victim instead of actually taking accountability for his horrendous actions.

“I was unfaithful to my partner during a very important time of our lives - an act I regret to this day. We are currently undergoing an unfortunate and painful separation which is being resolved through legal proceedings. As you can imagine, separations are quite a difficult thing to deal with, especially with a child involved.”

We previously reported that a young wife has faced the worst type of public humiliation from her husband after being allegedly caught in bed with another man in Karonga District of Malawi.

Reports reveal that the yet-to-be-identified husband was tipped off by friends about the escapades of the wife, who has reportedly been cheating on him for a long time. In anger, he sprung up and stormed into the room where his wife was in bed with with her lover.
