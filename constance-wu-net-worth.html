Cyberbullying can have serious consequences for its victims, impacting their mental and emotional well-being. It can lead to feelings of depression, anxiety, and even thoughts of self-harm. It is essential to create awareness about cyberbullying and work towards building a more inclusive and respectful online community. Support systems, such as therapy, can help individuals cope with the psychological effects of cyberbullying and find ways to heal.
