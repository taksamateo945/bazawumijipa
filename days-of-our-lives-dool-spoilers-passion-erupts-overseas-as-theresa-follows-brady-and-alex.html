Passion Erupts Overseas As Theresa Follows Brady And Alex

 Posted on February 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  442 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers tease that Theresa Donovan (Jenn Lilley) has only been in Salem a few days but has already stirred things up! Theresa arrived in Salem, saying she wanted to attend the memorial service for Victor Kiriakis (the late John Aniston) but it became quite clear almost immediately that it wasn’t the only reason.

She made a beeline for Brady Black (Eric Martsolf) and practically begged him to resume their long-dead romance.

Days of Our Lives Spoilers – Brady Black Wants Nothing To Do With Theresa Donovan!

But Brady pushed her away. He said that the two of them were toxic to each other and their only connection is their shared son. Theresa tried her best to sway Brady but he wasn’t having.

Theresa left in tears even as Brady continued to grow angrier by the second. Brady’s recent romantic failures with both Kristen DiMera (Stacey Haiduk) and Chloe Lane (Nadia Bjorlin) have seemingly darkened his memories of his time with Theresa because they were on much better terms when Theresa left Salem a few years ago.

Days of Our Lives Updates – Theresa Donovan And Alex Kiriakis Hit It Off Immediately!

White at the service for Victor, Theresa met Alex Kiriakis (Robert Scott Wilson) and the two of them hit it off. She asked Alex out to dinner right in front of Brady, likely hoping to make him jealous.

Regardless of whether that works or not, look for Alex and Theresa to form quite a connection. But even while they are getting to know each other, questions surrounding Victor’s actions before he died will begin to come to light.

Alex Kiriakis And Brady Black Head To Greece!

Wanting to get to the bottom of Victor’s sudden trip to Greece, look for Alex and Brady to head there very soon. There will likely be some huge revelations regarding Alex during this trip, but that doesn’t mean there won’t be passion well. With both Alex and Brady heading overseas, it seems likely that Theresa won’t pass up the opportunity to join them.

Regardless of what they find, look for emotions to run high. Theresa could very well end up in bed with either Brady or Alex as they reel from what they find.
