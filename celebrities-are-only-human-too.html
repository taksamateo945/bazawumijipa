Celebrities are only human too

 Posted on February 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  297 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Every time we read a newspaper or a magazine nine out of ten times it will be a negative, nasty and or untrue story about a celebrity.

These journalists are paid very good money to write the truth, so why is it that they like to spread negative vibes by writing horrible articles?.

Our society has enough problems with bullies and trolls on the internet and in schools etc, we do not need popular media outlets adding fuel to the fire and acting like it is OK to write cruel stuff about people just because they are a celebrity.

Being in the public eye does not mean they open themselves up to having a load of horrible untrue stuff written about them, they are only human the same as us.

We are all people with feelings, just because a person is on TV does not mean they don’t feel hurt when they see cruel things written about them.

Everyone has a duty to make the world a better place and to see major newspapers/magazines printing nasty content is very dangerous. It is showing young people that it is OK to body shame, it is OK to write lies about someone’s private life, it is OK to write cruel stuff about them because they are a celebrity. IT IS NOT OK.

Celebrities are just normal people with  extraordinary jobs, at the end of the day they go home, take a shower, eat some food and go to sleep, just like the rest of us. We are all human.

Some seasoned journalists are professional and nice people but sadly there are a lot out there who tear into people just because they are famous, this needs to STOP.

Everyone has feelings and no one deserved to be bullied.
